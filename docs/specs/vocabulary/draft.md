## Purpose

Defines *draft* as a formal state of a Blueprint: work in progress, not yet
released, freely modifiable, and not required to carry signatures. A Blueprint
transitions from draft to released through an explicit act that fixes its
content. Until released, a Blueprint's marks may be replaced, reordered, or
squashed. After release, it is immutable.
