**Status:** Draft — placeholder. Content not yet written.

## Purpose

States the contract every conforming authoring tool MUST satisfy: producing
well-formed Blueprints, attaching correct marks on modifications, preserving
marks and signatures produced by other tools, and reporting validation errors
in a standardized way.
