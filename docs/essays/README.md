# Blueprint Essays

This folder is the public trail of thinking behind the Blueprint specification. Each essay is dated, attributed, and written to be read in order by someone — a reporter, a regulator, a prospective adopter, a future collaborator — who needs to understand not only what Blueprint is but why every design decision looks the way it does.

The essays are not marketing. They are the record of the argument.

## Ordering

Essays are numbered in publication order. Numbering is stable: once an essay has a number it keeps that number even if later essays are inserted with decimal suffixes.

## Status

- **draft** — private, lives in `drafts/` (gitignored), not yet published.
- **published** — committed at the top level of this folder, dated, not revised in substance after publication. Corrections are appended, not rewritten.

## Conventions

- One essay per file. Filenames are `NN-slug.md`.
- Each essay opens with a YAML front matter block: `title`, `number`, `status`, `date`, `author`, `abstract`.
- Inline links to the specification are preferred over paraphrase; the essays exist to explain the specification, not to replace it.
- No client names, no proprietary engagements. Generic stand-ins only.
- The register is direct and serious. The essays are allowed to have a voice that the specification cannot.
